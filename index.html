About Me

My career began in communication and advertising, and grew into a 26-year journey across professional translation, subtitling, marketing research, and language education. Today, as a Software Development student at MITT, I'm bringing all this experience into the world of technology — creating digital tools that connect communication, learning, and design.

As a translator, I've worked with clients in fields ranging from business, health and finance to filmmaking and history. My background in marketing research has strengthened my understanding of audiences and how people interact with information.

As an English teacher and communication coach, I help professionals prepare for interviews, presentations, and workplace communication, designing tailored lessons that build clarity and confidence.

Now, I'm combining these strengths with software development to build thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences.
